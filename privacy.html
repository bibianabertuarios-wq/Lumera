Privacy Policy and Data Protection

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. INTRODUCTION

At Lumera, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information.

This policy complies with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation of the EU)
  • LOPD (Spanish Organic Law on Data Protection)
  • LSSI (Law on Information Society Services)

2. DATA CONTROLLER

Identity: Lumera
Contact email: privacy@lumera.app
Data Protection Officer: dpo@lumera.app

3. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?

3.1 INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

When you register:

  • Email address
  • Password (stored encrypted)
  • Name (optional)
  • Age or age range

When using the application:

  • Symptom information (sleep, energy, mood, hot flashes, etc.)
  • Food preferences and intolerances
  • Exercise restrictions
  • Wellness goals
  • Community messages (if you participate)
  • Conversations with LUMI (wellness guide)

Payment information:

  • Processed by our payment provider (Lemon Squeezy)
  • We do NOT store credit card data
  • We only receive payment confirmation and subscription status

3.2 AUTOMATIC TECHNICAL INFORMATION

When you use Lumera we collect:

  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • Browser used
  • Preferred language
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages visited within the application

4. HOW DO WE USE YOUR INFORMATION?

4.1 TO PROVIDE THE SERVICE

We use your data to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Personalize your menus according to your needs
  • Adapt exercises to your restrictions
  • Provide personalized responses through LUMI
  • Track your progress and symptoms
  • Process your subscription and payments

4.2 TO IMPROVE LUMERA

We use aggregated and anonymized data to:

  • Understand how the application is used
  • Identify errors and technical issues
  • Develop new features
  • Improve personalization algorithms

IMPORTANT: Aggregated data is completely anonymous and does not personally identify you.

5. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING (GDPR)

We process your personal data under the following legal bases:

5.1 CONTRACT PERFORMANCE

  • Provide the service you contracted
  • Manage your account and subscription

5.2 CONSENT

  • Send marketing communications (if you accept)
  • Use non-essential cookies (if you accept)
  • Share information in the community (if you participate)

5.3 LEGITIMATE INTEREST

  • Improve the service through aggregated analysis
  • Detect and prevent fraud
  • Conduct internal business analysis

6. WITH WHOM DO WE SHARE YOUR INFORMATION?

6.1 WE DO NOT SELL YOUR DATA

We NEVER sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for their marketing purposes.

6.2 SERVICE PROVIDERS

We share information ONLY with essential providers:

Supabase (Data Storage):

  • Securely stores your information
  • Location: Servers in the EU
  • GDPR compliant
  • Website: supabase.com

Lemon Squeezy (Payment Processing):

  • Processes subscription payments
  • PCI DSS and GDPR compliant
  • Website: lemonsqueezy.com

Anthropic (API for LUMI):

  • Processes your queries to LUMI
  • Does not store conversations after processing them
  • Complies with privacy standards
  • Website: anthropic.com

Vercel (Application Hosting):

  • Hosts the web application
  • Globally distributed servers
  • GDPR compliant
  • Website: vercel.com

7. WHERE IS YOUR DATA STORED?

7.1 PRIMARY LOCATION

Your data is primarily stored in:

  • Servers in the European Union (Supabase)
  • Complies with GDPR requirements

7.2 INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

Some providers (such as Anthropic for LUMI) may process data outside the EU:

  • Only with providers that guarantee adequate protection
  • Through standard contractual clauses approved by the EU
  • Or under valid transfer frameworks

8. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR DATA?

8.1 ACTIVE ACCOUNT

While your account is active, we maintain all your information to provide the service.

8.2 AFTER CANCELLATION

If you cancel your subscription:

  • We maintain your inactive account for 90 days
  • In case you decide to reactivate it
  • You can request immediate deletion at any time

8.3 AFTER ACCOUNT DELETION

If you delete your account:

  • Personal data deleted within 30 days
  • Billing data retained 6 years (legal tax obligation)
  • Anonymized data may be kept indefinitely

9. SECURITY OF YOUR DATA

9.1 SECURITY MEASURES

We implement technical and organizational measures:

Encryption:

  • Data in transit: TLS/SSL (HTTPS)
  • Data at rest: AES-256 encryption
  • Passwords: Hashing with bcrypt

Limited access:

  • Only authorized personnel
  • Two-factor authentication for administrators
  • Access audit logs

Secure infrastructure:

  • Firewalls and DDoS protection
  • Regular and encrypted backups
  • 24/7 security monitoring

10. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

10.1 RIGHT OF ACCESS

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Know how we process your information
  • Receive a response within 30 days

10.2 RIGHT OF RECTIFICATION

  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Update your information at any time

10.3 RIGHT TO ERASURE ("Right to be forgotten")

  • Request deletion of your data
  • Applicable in certain circumstances
  • Except if we have a legal obligation to keep them

10.4 RIGHT TO DATA PORTABILITY

  • Receive your data in structured format (JSON/CSV)
  • Transfer data to another service
  • Free and in machine-readable format

10.5 RIGHT TO OBJECT

  • Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Object to direct marketing (at any time)

10.6 RIGHT TO LODGE A COMPLAINT

You can file a complaint with:

  • Spanish Data Protection Agency (AEPD)
  • C/ Jorge Juan, 6, 28001 Madrid
  • Website: www.aepd.es

11. HOW TO EXERCISE YOUR RIGHTS

To exercise any of your rights:

Email: privacy@lumera.app
Subject: "GDPR Request - [Your Right]"

Include:

  • Your full name
  • Your account email
  • Clear description of your request
  • Copy of identification (to verify identity)

Response time:

  • We will confirm receipt within 48 hours
  • We will respond completely within 30 days
  • Extension to 60 days if complex (we will inform you)

12. MINORS

Lumera is NOT directed at people under 18 years of age:

  • We do not intentionally collect data from minors
  • If we discover that a minor has registered, we will delete their account
  • Parents/guardians can contact us to report misuse

13. ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND LUMI

13.1 QUERY PROCESSING

LUMI (our wellness guide) uses AI from Anthropic:

  • Your queries are sent to Anthropic for processing
  • They are NOT stored permanently after responding to you
  • They are processed confidentially and securely

13.2 DATA USED

LUMI temporarily accesses:

  • Your name (if you provided it)
  • Context of your current query
  • Information necessary to respond

Does NOT access: Your entire symptom history or complete personal data without your permission.

We may update this policy:

  • Significant changes will also be announced in the application
  • The "last updated" date will be modified
